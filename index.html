What I teach

From first chord shapes to advanced modern fingerstyle.

Whether you’re just starting as an adult beginner or already play confidently, we’ll build a path that fits your taste in music and your level.

Modern fingerstyle foundations

Thumb independence, percussive hits, harmonics and fluid patterns that make solo guitar sound full and orchestral.

Melodic improvisation

Improvise tastefully over chord progressions using simple, musical ideas — not endless scale drills.

Instrumental covers of songs you love

Take your favorite songs and arrange them for solo guitar — combining bass, chords and melody in one part.
