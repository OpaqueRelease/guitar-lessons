About the teacher

Fingerstyle guitar teacher in Ljubljana.

The teacher works with advanced guitarists and motivated adult beginners who want to go deeper into modern fingerstyle, improvisation and writing their own instrumental music.

  • Focused on modern fingerstyle

    Techniques and musical ideas inspired by contemporary fingerstyle players – not just traditional classical or basic strumming.

What students learn

From first chord shapes to advanced modern fingerstyle.

Whether a student is just starting as an adult beginner or already plays confidently, the lessons follow a path that fits their taste in music and their level.

Modern fingerstyle foundations

Thumb independence, percussive hits, harmonics and fluid patterns that make solo guitar sound full and orchestral.

Melodic improvisation

Improvise tastefully over chord progressions using simple, musical ideas — not endless scale drills.

Instrumental covers of songs students love

Arranging students’ favorite songs for solo guitar — combining bass, chords and melody in one part.
